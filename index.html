Questions?

Frequently Asked.

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, grading, and business with Kabendera.

How do I place an order?

Browse our product categories above, click "Ask" on any bale that interests you, and we'll connect via WhatsApp within 30 minutes. Share your order details, and we'll handle the rest—payment, logistics, delivery tracking. No complicated forms, just direct communication.

What are bale grades (Grade A, B, Premium)?

Premium Grade: Pristine condition, like new. Minimal wear. Perfect for boutiques.
Grade A: Excellent condition, light wear. Ideal for retail resale.
Grade B: Good condition, moderate wear. Budget-friendly for bulk reselling.

All bales are pre-inspected. Mixed grades provide excellent value for high-volume resellers.

How much is shipping, and how fast?

Nairobi: Free delivery for orders 3+ bales. 1-2 business days.
Upcountry: Flat rate KSh 2,500-4,000 depending on location. 2-4 business days via Jatco/ACD.
Bulk orders (10+): Negotiate custom logistics rates. Contact us for quote.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

5-9 bales: 5% discount on total order.
10-19 bales: 10% discount + free shipping.
20+ bales: 15% discount + free shipping + exclusive early access.

Volume buyers qualify for exclusive bales and first pick on new stock. Contact our wholesale team for custom deals.

What's included in each bale?

Each bale contains approximately 100-250 pieces depending on category. Contents include clothing items, accessories, and sometimes home goods—all sorted by type (e.g., "Ladies Dress" = mixed styles, colors, sizes). We don't mix categories, so "Kids" stays kids, "Sports" stays sports.

Can I return or exchange bales?

We guarantee accurate grading and description. If a bale doesn't match the advertised grade/condition upon delivery, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll either replace it or issue a full refund. For bulk orders, we can arrange pre-inspection video calls before payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

M-Pesa: 0712 345 678 (instant confirmation)
Bank Transfer: Kabendera Ltd, KCB, Acc: [Account Number]
Cash: Available for Nairobi pick-ups at our warehouse
Installment: For orders 10+ bales, we offer flexible payment plans

I'm just starting out—where do I begin?

Start small: Order 2-3 bales to test the market (e.g., 1 Ladies Dress + 1 Sports).
Talk to us: WhatsApp after your first order. We'll give you personalized advice based on your location and target market.
Grow with us: Once you find what works, scale up. We remember loyal customers and prioritize their orders.
